# Content Contract

## Theme

Choose one emotionally specific moment, such as Sunday-night anxiety, feeling left behind, not knowing how to rest, or quietly rebuilding after disappointment. Avoid generic themes such as “life,” “growth,” or “healing” without a concrete scene.

## Seven-Card Arc

1. Cover: name the reader and promise a small emotional shift.
2. Recognition: describe the moment accurately.
3. Permission: remove one unnecessary demand or self-judgment.
4. Reframe: offer a useful distinction, not a slogan.
5. Small action: suggest one action that can be done today.
6. Companionship: make the reader feel less alone without inventing a personal story.
7. Closing: leave one memorable line and a low-pressure invitation to save, share, or comment.

Do not let adjacent cards repeat the same event or conclusion. Each inner card needs an original headline and a separate supporting line.

## Voice

- Write in natural Chinese by default.
- Be warm, restrained, concrete, and non-preachy.
- Prefer “你可以先……” over “你必须……”.
- Avoid exaggerated internet language, fake intimacy, empty encouragement, and diagnostic labels.
- Keep cover text scannable and inner-card copy short enough to breathe.

## Original and Sourced Text

Default every card to `source_type: original`.

Use `source_type: sourced` only when the exact line, author, and named work have been verified. Record the source URL or bibliographic note in `album-source-audit.md`. Short translations must be labeled as translations. If verification is uncertain, remove the attribution and write a new original line.

## Visual Direction

- Use one new photo concept per card.
- Keep environment, sky, room, water, or street at 75-90% and people at 5-15%.
- Prefer believable natural light, restrained 35mm grain, quiet negative space, and everyday scenes.
- Generate photos without text, logos, watermarks, app interfaces, or brand marks.
- Keep all seven cards in one color and grain family while varying location, action, and framing.

## Post Copy

- Provide three title directions and identify one recommendation.
- Keep the caption at roughly 80-220 Chinese characters unless asked otherwise.
- Use one primary objective and one CTA.
- Add 5-8 relevant, non-duplicated topics; never exceed 10.
- Do not default to private-message or sales CTAs.

# Privacy and Safety

Before handing off or sharing a package:

1. Remove names, handles, contact details, addresses, location metadata, faces, license plates, private analytics, comments, and conversation fragments unless the user explicitly intends and has the right to publish them.
2. Never store passwords, API keys, tokens, cookies, session exports, browser profiles, or authentication screenshots.
3. Use relative paths in specs and manifests. Do not preserve usernames or home-directory paths.
4. Do not bundle source archives, output history, logs, caches, temporary files, or editor metadata.
5. Do not implement platform login, upload, draft, scheduling, publishing, commenting, messaging, or evasion behavior.
6. Treat every third-party image, font, quote, template, and code fragment as restricted until its license or permission is verified.

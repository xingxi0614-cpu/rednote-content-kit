## Dated calendar cards

- `calendar-2026-07-22.png`: ordinary dated card, 1242 × 1656.
- `calendar-2026-07-23-solar-term.png`: exact-day Major Heat card, 1242 × 1656.

The Gregorian dates, weekdays, lunar labels, and solar-term label were checked in the original production workflow. The short lines come from classical works by Gao Pian and Bai Juyi; the scenery was generated locally.

## No-date heartfelt albums

`photo-paper-album-cover.png` is the 1242 × 1656 photo-paper cover of the original album *Finish This Summer Slowly*. Its headline and subtitle are original editorial copy with no external quotation.

The `freedom-album-*.png` files are four selected outputs from the original album *Not Every Step Needs an Answer*:

- `freedom-album-overview.png`: overview of one cover and six inner cards, 1440 × 980.
- `freedom-album-cover.png`: selected 3:4 cover, 1242 × 1656.
- `freedom-album-slide-01.png`: selected 3:4 inner card, 1242 × 1656.
- `freedom-album-slide-06.png`: selected 3:4 inner card, 1242 × 1656.

The scenery was produced through the local image-generation workflow. The Chinese album copy shown here is original editorial text with no external quotation or author attribution.
